My Favorite Movies

Top 3:

1. August Rush

August Rush has an amazing cast and is a heartfelt story. Robin Williams plays the antagonist, and delivers an amazing performance as a deadbeat musician using kids to hustle people for money via music.

2. Across The Universe

Across the Universe is based in the 1970s during the war where men were getting drafted and a group of friends were facing struggles with romance and psychedllics, to the music of the famous group the Beatles.

3. Interstellar

Interstellar is an award winning movie about the end of time on earth for humans. A group of NASA astronauts leave the planet and enter a wormhole that was believed to be placed by aliens trying to help them. They lose many crew members during this process and a simple trip through the wormhole costs them 23 years. In the end, they find out that the only ones helping them were themselves, and that the technology was later developed by the main protaganists daughter, in the future.
